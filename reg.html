Process to apply for New Licence

The process in brief:

Fill the online form from DOTM website. You'll get a verification page along with time. In case the page doesnot open, wait until the DOTM Server is up. We see most problem with DOTM website caused by a lot of triffic at same time. The problem possibly seen for the few days after DOTM open up registration. And you won't be able to fill mannal form these days. List of License Issue Office for Bagmati Zone are: Jagati-Bhaktapur, Thulobharayang-Kathmandu, Chabhil-Kathmandu, and Bagmati-Lalitpur.

2. Print the confirmation page and take along your national identity card (citizenship) and visit the DOTM office. The date of visit is mentioned in confirmation page.

3. Your citizenship as well as biometric details will be recorded there. Once done you'll be advised to get your eyes and blood group checked. (Eyes and blood test service is available near DOTM office).

4. Now you should pay Nrs.500 for a written exam. Usually, the exam is next day 7:00 am. In case you have any licence (bike, car or any) and applying for add category like a car, you don't need to take the written exam. In general sense, if you have any vehicle licence you don't need to attain written exam.

5. Once you pass the exam you will be able to attend for vehicle trial next day (bike or car or any you intend to apply for). They'll inform where and when to attend. Bike on rent costs Rs.50 and Rs.500 for car hire on the trial centre.(Only for Bagmati-Lalitpur)



6. Once you pass trial you should visit DOTM office and pay revenue Rs.1500 after 10 working days (In case of new license). For add category you just need to pay Rs.500. You'll receive a payment slip along with license number. Also, they'll inform about approx time to collection your license. Till then you will be able to run vehicle using the payment slip (Rajaswa Paper).



7. Visit DOTM Office with slip in the time mentioned. The slip works as licence temporarily. You'll have your driving license soon.

Driving License Issuing Information and Written Exam Result

Here is the process to get the driving license issue information:
Forward an SMS/message LC_space_Applicant ID to 31003
You will get a reply if your licences are printed ready or not.

For the written exam result of driving license, You need to
-Forward an SMS/message WT_space_Applicaton ID to 31003
Likewise, you will get the update of you passing or failing in written test. Your total cost
Rs.500 for the exam fee.
Rs.100 approx for eye and blood test.
Rs.50 bike charge in the trial and for the car Rs.500.
Rs.1500 to be paid after you pass all exams [For new Licence].
Rs.500 to be paid for [Add Category]
Grand Total: Approx. Rs. 2150/-

Don't forget to take your Citizenship, Licence (In case of Add Category), Passport Size Photo, Driving School Certificate for Cars and other heavy vehicles, Blood Group Card and Some Extra Cash just in case.
